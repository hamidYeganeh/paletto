# Code of Conduct

We aim to provide a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers.
